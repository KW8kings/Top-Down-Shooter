Controls:

W or Upward Arrow: the character moves a step forward.
S or Downward Arrow: the character moves backstep or to its original position.
D or Right Arrow: the character moves to the right side.
A or Left Arrow: the character moves to the left side.
R: enables the character to shift to the other side.
Clicking The Left Mouse: enables the character to fire his weapon or gun.
